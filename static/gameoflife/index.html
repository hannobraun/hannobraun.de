This is Conway's Game of Life! Place cells by clicking into the box to the left. Then click on Start Simulation below the box.

The simulation works according to 4 simple rules:

  1. A cell with fewer than two neighbours dies.
  2. A cell with two or three neighbours lives on.
  3. A cell with more than three neighbours dies.
  4. On a grid position with exactly three neighbours, a new cell is placed.

Place some cells and try it out! If what you're seeing is not interesting enough, try to place more cells. Don't spread them out too much!
